DONATE

SkillsUSA is a 501(c)3 organization and our chapter relies heavily on donations from contributors that help our students grow and develop into future leaders, tradespeople, and impactful members of their community. If you are interested in donating to our chapter of SkillsUSA, each dollar will go a long way in supporting our chapter’s dedication to help our community.

If you are interested in donating, please send a check to:

Lynn Tech SkillsUSA 80 Neptune Blvd. Lynn, MA 01902
