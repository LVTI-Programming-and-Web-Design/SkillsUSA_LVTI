Our Mission!

Our chapter of SkillsUSA is committed to ensuring excellence in all areas of a student’s life. Whether it is promoting career and technical education, guiding individual leadership skills, or providing service to the community of Lynn, we are invested in the development of the whole student and person. We are committed to developing a student’s personal, workplace, and technical skills grounded in academics.
